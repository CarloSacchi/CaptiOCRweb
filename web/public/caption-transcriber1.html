Frequently Asked Questions About Caption Transcription

How accurate is CaptiOCR at transcribing captions?

CaptiOCR achieves high accuracy when transcribing captions because it captures the already-processed text displayed on your screen, rather than trying to interpret audio. This means it's not affected by accents, background noise, or audio quality issues that plague speech-to-text solutions.

Does CaptiOCR work with all video conferencing platforms?

Yes, CaptiOCR works with any platform that displays captions on your screen, including Microsoft Teams, Zoom, Google Meet, Webex, GoToMeeting, and others. It doesn't rely on platform-specific APIs, making it universally compatible.

How does CaptiOCR handle speaker identification?

CaptiOCR captures speaker information exactly as it appears in the captions. Most platforms include the speaker's name before their text, which CaptiOCR preserves in its transcription, making it easy to follow who said what.

Is my meeting content secure when using CaptiOCR?

Absolutely. CaptiOCR processes everything locally on your device. No audio, video, or text content is ever sent to external servers or the cloud. This makes it ideal for confidential or sensitive discussions.

Can I edit the transcribed captions after capturing?

Yes, CaptiOCR saves transcripts as plain text files that you can open and edit with any text editor. This makes it easy to clean up any minor errors or format the content for specific needs.

What languages does CaptiOCR support for caption transcription?

CaptiOCR currently supports caption transcription in English, Italian, French, Spanish, German, and Portuguese. Additional languages will be added in future updates.
